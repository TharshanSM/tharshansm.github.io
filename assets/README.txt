PLACE TWO FILES IN THIS FOLDER
==============================

1) Your CV  ->  assets/Ugatharshan_Murugesu_CV.pdf
   The "Download CV" buttons link to this exact filename.
   (Just copy your PDF here and rename it to match, or update the
    links in index.html if you prefer a different name.)

2) (Optional) Your photo  ->  assets/profile.jpg
   To use it, open index.html, find the "hero__portrait" block,
   and replace the <span class="hero__monogram">UM</span> line with:

       <img src="assets/profile.jpg" alt="Ugatharshan Murugesu" />

   Until then, a stylised "UM" monogram is shown instead.
